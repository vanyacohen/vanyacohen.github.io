CAPE introduces a novel approach for resolving precondition errors in task planning for robotic agents by leveraging large language models (LLMs). The method re-prompts LLMs using error feedback, allowing robots to make corrective actions in real-world environments. We implement CAPE on a Spot robot and demonstrate improvements over prior open-loop planning methods.
